    @ 2017 Robogame at USTC      Advisor: Prof. Lu Huang

        Making a robot which can draw almost everything if you input its black-and-white photograph and can recognize the characters in the photo and write it in another typeface called Xiaozhuan, an ancient style of calligraphy.

    This project won the second prize at Robogame2017 -- a robot competition at USTC.
